Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

MyVerba ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

MyVerba is designed to store your personal vocabulary collections locally on your device.

The App may store the following information:

Dictionaries created by you

Words, translations, meanings, notes, and learning statistics

App settings and preferences

This information is stored locally on your device.

MyVerba does not require account registration and does not upload your vocabulary data to external servers.

File Access

The App may request access to files on your device for the following purposes:

Importing vocabulary lists

Exporting backups or dictionaries

Sharing exported files

The App only accesses files that you explicitly select.

Advertising

The free version of MyVerba may display advertisements provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect certain information automatically, including:

Device information

Advertising identifiers

App interaction data

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics and Crash Reports

At this time, MyVerba does not use analytics or crash reporting services.

If such services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Security

Your vocabulary data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.

However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Users are encouraged to regularly export backups of their data.

Children's Privacy

MyVerba is not directed to children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
